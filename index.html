Welcome to my hometown, Bangalore!

The IT capital of India, this city is the home and birthplace of a large number of MNCs and start-ups.

Tourist places around Bangalore

Nandi Hills

Located 60km from Bangalore. this place is famous for its scenic sunrise. Tourists come here to enjoy a day-long family picnic.

Shivasamudram Falls

Located 130km from Bangalore, this place is famous for its beautiful twin waterfalls on the Kaveri river.

Belur

Located 220km from Bangalore, this place is famous for the temple, which houses intricate sculptures dating back to the 12th century.
